ABOUT THE CREW

Built by electricians, run like a business

4 Tec Electric has handled commercial electrical work across the Fort Worth area since 2016 — mostly ground-up builds and remodels for general contractors, with residential jobs taken on rarely.

Every hire goes through a face-to-face interview before they set foot on a job site, and every contractor works under a clear payout agreement. It's a small crew that's stayed small on purpose — nine people who know the work, not a call center routing you to whoever's free.

For sixteen years, jobs have been won and run the same way: a direct phone call to someone who picks up.
